# Curriculum vitae

## Professional experience

**2025-present | Doctoral researcher | Wageningen University**

As a doctoral researcher, I study the effects of extreme weather on global agricultural economics (supply, prices and trade). The main contributions of my PhD are in addressing methodological challenges associated with machine learning,  econometrics and (trade) network analysis.

**2022-2025 | Data scientist | Wageningen Socio-Economic Research**

Conducting quantitative research in the Dutch and European Agri-sector.
My role in research projects often related to data analysis activities, such as
extraction, cleaning, visualization and performing statistical analysis using
both linear models as well as advanced machine learning techniques.

## Education

**2021-2023 | MSc. Econometrics | Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam**

MSc Econometrics with a specialisation in Climate Economics. Thesis subject: Effects of drought and its interactions on crop yields in the Netherlands.

**2017-2021 | BSc. Economics | Wageningen University**

Economic BSc, with a large focus on sustainability and climate. Included a minor in Applied Econometrics at the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam. Thesis subject: The role of seasonality in stationarity and cointegration analysis.
